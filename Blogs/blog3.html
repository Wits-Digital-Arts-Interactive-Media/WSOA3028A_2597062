Week 3 Blog Post

Alignment

User Alignment - designing and structuring my website to meet the needs, expectations, and behaviors of the target users.

My current main goal is to create a website as a new platform for my art as a portfolio and product. Additionally, I aim to achieve a good mark for my interactive media class, planning to have a responsive, accessible, and concise artistic website.

Ultimately, I want my users to see my art and convey it in a creative way that highlights the art of web design.

My Users

What I want my users to do:

  • To view my portfolio.
  • To engage with my art, understanding its significance and meaning.
  • To contact me for commissions and collaborations.
  • To connect with my social media.
  • To purchase artwork.
  • To be aware of any potential events or projects I am involved in or working on.

Who is my target audience?

As an artist, my target audience for my website could include art enthusiasts, collectors, potential clients, galleries, and fans of my work. They are likely on my website to explore and appreciate my artwork, learn more about me as an artist, and potentially purchase or commission artwork.

What do they want to see?

My audience wants to see a visually appealing and well-organized portfolio showcasing my artwork. They may also be interested in learning about my artistic process, inspirations, and upcoming events or exhibitions. Additionally, they may want to easily contact me for inquiries or collaborations.
