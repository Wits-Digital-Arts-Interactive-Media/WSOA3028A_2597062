Welcome to Djibraeel's Website

Hi, I am Djibraeel Morojele. I am currently a student at Wits University. This website is part of an ongoing Interactive media assignment, where I intend on making a full website depicting my portfolio as a student and as an artist. I will be posting blog posts based on the topics I am learning in web design. My portfolio will depict my artworks across media, and I will also showcase my essays and assignments. I aim to make this a fully fledged artist website where I can communicate my artwork, provide contact for commissions and collaborations, and as a means to purchase art. I hope you enjoy this website and find it as clear and creative as I wish it to be.
